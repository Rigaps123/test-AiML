How NLP Works

NLP includes different kinds of techniques used to process data. It encompasses a wide variety of methods such as statistical and machine learning (ML) techniques, rule-based, and algorithm approaches. Human language may vary across the verbal and written channels which makes it important for NLP to include different kinds of approaches for interpretation. Its primary task is to break down the language to understand how all the parts work together to generate meaning.
